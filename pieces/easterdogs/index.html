Easter Dogs - Mini Game

Home

I created this to allow my wife to enjoy an easter egg hunt during lockdown.

The game starts with a simulated Whatsapp chat, just enter your name to play.

The Whatsapp chat is timed, but once it completes, it takes you into the game.

This uses leaflet to provide a map, and markers above above a specific zoom level.

The first clue can be found on Stave Hill, SE16. Zoom the map to get the first marker to appear.
